About Stixs

Welcome to Stixs Asian Kitchen

Located in the heart of Newcastle on St Andrew's Street, Stixs brings you an exciting fusion of authentic Korean, Japanese and Taiwanese flavours. Our open-kitchen restaurant specialises in Korean fried chicken, 18-hour tonkotsu broth and street-food favourites.

From crispy 5-piece wings to volcano tonkotsu ramen and galbi kimchi rice bowls, every dish is crafted with passion and fresh ingredients.

Why Choose Stixs?

  • Authentic Asian flavours
  • 18-hour pork broth & daily fresh noodles
  • Allergy-conscious menu
  • Friendly, knowledgeable staff
